About Me

👋 Who am I?

Hi! I'm Theo, a Brazilian developer passionate about recreating lost media, creating Minecraft mods, and developing games on Roblox. This website is where I share my journey, projects, and everything I'm working on.

I founded Classic Studios with two friends. Together, we started developing games under the Classic Studios name.

📅 May 2026

I began my first lost media recreation project, Barguy Remake, for Android.

📅 June 2026

I started developing the Minecraft Super Speed Mod and another lost media recreation project.
