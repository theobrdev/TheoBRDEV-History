Scorefeud Remake

Lost Media Recreation

📋 Project Information

Status: 🔵 In Development

Platform: Android

Engine: MIT App Inventor

Developer: TBloper

Original Game: Scorefeud / Scorefeud LITE (DVloper, 2012)

📖 About

Scorefeud Remake is a recreation of the lost media games Scorefeud and Scorefeud LITE, originally created by DVloper in 2012. The goal is to preserve these games and make them playable again on modern Android devices.
